Welcome to Balapedia, a fan-made wiki for Balatro — a poker-themed roguelike deck-building game developed by LocalThunk and published by Playstack. Build powerful synergies, discover joker combos, and beat the blinds to win. This wiki covers jokers, blinds, tags, poker hands, decks, vouchers, stakes, and antes.

For more information, visit the Balatro Steam Page or the Balatro Fandom Wiki.

jokers

There are 150 jokers in Balatro across 4 rarities: Common (60), Uncommon (65), Rare (20), and Legendary (5). Shop appearance rates are 70% Common, 25% Uncommon, and 5% Rare. Legendary jokers can only be obtained through The Soul spectral card.

  4. Crimson Heart - One random Joker is disabled each hand
  5. Cerulean Bell - Forces 1 card to always be selected

tags

Tags are rewards obtained when you skip a Blind. Each tag provides a different bonus.

  • Uncommon Tag - Shop has a free Uncommon Joker
  • Rare Tag - Shop has a free Rare Joker
  • Negative Tag - Next base edition shop Joker is free and becomes Negative
  • Foil Tag - Next base edition shop Joker is free and becomes Foil
  • Holographic Tag - Next base edition shop Joker is free and becomes Holographic
  • Polychrome Tag - Next base edition shop Joker is free and becomes Polychrome
  • Investment Tag - After defeating the Boss Blind, gain $25
  • Voucher Tag - Adds a Voucher to the next shop
  • Boss Tag - Rerolls the Boss Blind
  • Standard Tag - Gives a free Standard Pack
  • Charm Tag - Gives a free Arcana Pack
  • Meteor Tag - Gives a free Celestial Pack
  • Buffoon Tag - Gives a free Buffoon Pack
  • Handy Tag - Gives $1 per hand played this run
  • Garbage Tag - Gives $1 per unused discard this run
  • Ethereal Tag - Gives a free Spectral Pack
  • Coupon Tag - Initial cards in next shop are free
  • Double Tag - Gives a copy of the next selected Tag
  • Juggle Tag - +3 hand size next round
  • D6 Tag - Starts next shop with a free reroll
  • Top-up Tag - Create up to 2 Common Jokers
  • Speed Tag - Gives $5 per Ante skipped this run
  • Orbital Tag - Upgrade a poker hand by 3 levels
  • Economy Tag - Doubles your money (max $40)

decks

There are 15 decks in Balatro. Each deck modifies your starting conditions. The Red, Blue, Yellow, and Green decks are unlocked from the start. Others are unlocked through gameplay achievements.

  • Red Deck - +1 discard each round
  • Blue Deck - +1 hand each round
  • Yellow Deck - Start with +$10
  • Green Deck - No Interest earned; earn $2 per remaining hand and $1 per remaining discard at end of round
  • Black Deck - +1 Joker slot; -1 hand each round
  • Magic Deck - Start with the Crystal Ball voucher and 2 copies of The Fool tarot card
  • Nebula Deck - Start with the Telescope voucher; -1 consumable slot
  • Ghost Deck - Spectral cards may appear in the shop; start with a free Spectral Pack
  • Abandoned Deck - Start with no face cards (all Jacks, Queens, and Kings removed from deck)
  • Checkered Deck - Start with 26 Spades and 26 Hearts (no Clubs or Diamonds)
  • Zodiac Deck - Start with Tarot Merchant, Planetary Merchant, and Overstock vouchers
  • Painted Deck - +2 hand size; -1 Joker slot
  • Anaglyph Deck - After defeating each Boss Blind, get a Double Tag
  • Plasma Deck - Balance Chips and Mult when calculating score, then double the result; no interest earned
  • Erratic Deck - All card ranks and suits in the deck are randomized

shop

The shop appears after every blind. It has 2 card slots, 2 booster pack slots, and 1 voucher slot. Rerolling costs $5, increasing by $1 per reroll — resets each new shop. Interest pays $1 per $5 held at end of round, capped at $5 (raised by Seed Money / Money Tree).

Card Slot Contents

  • Joker — 71.4% chance (Common 70% · Uncommon 25% · Rare 5%)
  • Tarot Card — 14.3% chance
  • Planet Card — 14.3% chance
  • Playing Card — available with Magic Trick voucher
  • Spectral Card — available with Ghost Deck or Omen Globe voucher

Joker Prices

  • Common — $1–$6
  • Uncommon — $4–$8
  • Rare — $7–$10
  • Legendary — only obtainable via The Soul spectral card (not purchasable)

Editions

  • Foil — +$2 to base price · +50 Chips
  • Holographic — +$3 to base price · +10 Mult
  • Polychrome — +$4 to base price · X1.5 Mult
  • Negative — +$5 to base price · +1 Joker slot

Booster Packs

  • Standard Pack — Playing cards added to your deck · Normal $4 (pick 1 of 3) · Jumbo $6 (pick 1 of 5) · Mega $8 (pick 2 of 5)
  • Arcana Pack — Tarot cards · Normal $4 (pick 1 of 3) · Jumbo $6 (pick 1 of 5) · Mega $8 (pick 2 of 5)
  • Celestial Pack — Planet cards · Normal $4 (pick 1 of 3) · Jumbo $6 (pick 1 of 5) · Mega $8 (pick 2 of 5)
  • Buffoon Pack — Joker cards · Normal $4 (pick 1 of 2) · Jumbo $6 (pick 1 of 4) · Mega $8 (pick 2 of 4)
  • Spectral Pack — Spectral cards · Normal $4 (pick 1 of 2) · Jumbo $6 (pick 1 of 4) · Mega $8 (pick 2 of 4)

vouchers

There are 32 vouchers in Balatro, sold in 16 base/upgrade pairs. Each costs $10. Buying a base voucher unlocks its upgrade in future shops. Each entry shows: Base → Upgrade.

  • Overstock - +1 card slot in shop (3 total)
    ↳ Overstock Plus - +1 more card slot (4 total)
  • Clearance Sale - All cards and packs in shop are 25% off
    ↳ Liquidation - 50% off
  • Hone - Foil, Holographic, and Polychrome cards appear 2× more often
    ↳ Glow Up - 4× more often
  • Reroll Surplus - Rerolls cost $2 less
    ↳ Reroll Glut - Rerolls cost an additional $2 less ($4 total)
  • Crystal Ball - +1 Consumable slot
    ↳ Omen Globe - Spectral cards may appear in Arcana Packs
  • Telescope - Celestial Packs always contain the Planet card for your most played hand
    ↳ Observatory - Each Planet card used gives its hand X1.5 Mult permanently
  • Grabber - +1 hand per round permanently
    ↳ Nacho Tong - +1 more hand per round (+2 total)
  • Wasteful - +1 discard per round permanently
    ↳ Recyclomancy - +1 more discard per round (+2 total)
  • Tarot Merchant - Tarot cards appear 2× more often in shop
    ↳ Tarot Tycoon - 4× more often
  • Planet Merchant - Planet cards appear 2× more often in shop
    ↳ Planet Tycoon - 4× more often
  • Seed Money - Raise interest cap per round to $10
    ↳ Money Tree - Raise interest cap to $20
  • Blank - Does nothing
    ↳ Antimatter - +1 Joker slot
  • Magic Trick - Playing cards can be purchased from the shop
    ↳ Illusion - Shop playing cards may also have Enhancements, Editions, and/or Seals
  • Hieroglyph - −1 Ante, −1 hand size per round
    ↳ Petroglyph - −1 Ante again, −1 discard per round
  • Director's Cut - Reroll the Boss Blind once per Ante for $10
    ↳ Retcon - Reroll Boss Blind unlimited times for $10
  • Paint Brush - +1 hand size
    ↳ Palette - +1 hand size again (+2 total)

stakes

Stakes are difficulty modifiers that increase the challenge of a run. Each stake adds a new modifier on top of all previous ones. Higher stakes unlock cosmetic rewards.

  • White Stake - Base difficulty; no extra modifiers
  • Red Stake - Small Blind gives no reward money
  • Green Stake - Required score scales faster each Ante
  • Black Stake - 30% chance for Jokers in shops and packs to have an Eternal sticker (cannot be sold)
  • Blue Stake - -1 Discard each round
  • Purple Stake - Required score scales even faster each Ante
  • Orange Stake - 30% chance for Jokers in shops and packs to have a Perishable sticker (debuffed after 5 rounds)
  • Gold Stake - 30% chance for Jokers in shops and packs to have a Rental sticker (costs $3 per round)
